Fast and Style

Drifting is considered one of the most difficult motorsports that require elite-level driving skills. It is also one of the most exhilarating motorsports to watch. Drifting involves taking quick turns with minimal loss of speed and negotiating turns and bends with the car going sideways, creating as much smoke as possible in the process.
